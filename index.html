About

I am a UX/UI designer with a strong foundation in visual design and a fascination with code.
For the past eight years I have worked to help people in the arts and non-profit sectors tell their stories. My experiences in photography, letterpress printing, and publication design have helped inform my visual vocabulary.
This past year I taught myself to code and began focusing my attention on design research and UX methodology. I wanted my work to have a greater impact.
In March of 2016 I was selected from a competitive pool to participate in the StartUp Institute Boston Web Design track. There I continue to expand my capabilities in User Research, Rapid Prototyping and Ideation, and Front-end Development.
